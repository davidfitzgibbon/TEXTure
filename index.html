TEXTure

The Problem

Jason Santa Maria gave a great talk at Build Web designers are very enthusiastic. It's why we stay up till all hours of the night trying to make things "just right" on our projects. However, this means that we look too closesly, and get lost in the details. We can't see the wood from the trees.

Jason Santa Maria gave a great talk at Build talking about web typography, he explained the problem. Our users don't see our sites like we do. They don't see the details. The don't see the little choices that we make as designers.

The Solution

TEXTure is a bookmarklet that allows you to blur your page. It lets you view your text, almost like a wireframe.
