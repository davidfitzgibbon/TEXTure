Texture

Jason Santa Maria gave a great talk at Build. While talking about web typography, he explained that users don't see our sites like we do. They don't see the details. The don't see the little choices that we make as designers.

Texture lets you see this blur. Like a first time visitor. At the click of a button.

Texture is a javascript bookmarklet that lets see a website like a new person, in the click of a button! Just click the link below, or drag it to your bookmarks bar to use it on any site!
